Shelter for a family

Floods, tidal surges and cyclones make many people homeless in remote and marginal areas every year. Many are also homeless due to river erosion. Many of them do not have the ability to build a small house. We build their houses with your donations.

Education for a child

Education is everyone's right. But not all children can go to school if they want to. We arrange for the education of such orphans with the help of your donations. At the same time, we provide accommodation to those who do not have accommodation.
